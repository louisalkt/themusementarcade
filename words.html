Word of the Week

a small etymology and note

winklepicker

(noun) — originally a name for a style of shoe with a sharp pointed toe; delightfully ridiculous, and delightful to say aloud.

Notes: why winklepicker? Because of the snails, of course.

Past words: dord, forsooth.
