Wise of the Week

tiny rules, carried lightly

  • Never eat yellow snow.
  • Never go into a forest with a dodgy tummy.
  • Neither toilet paper nor toilets themselves are naturally occurring. There is nothing more humiliating than wiping with a leaf.

A small collection of wises — silly, practical, and occasionally mortifying. Add them to your pocket of rules and consult when necessary.
